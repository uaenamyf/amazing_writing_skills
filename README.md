## English

**Amazing Writing Skills** is a comprehensive collection of 5 specialized AI-agent skills designed to assist researchers through every stage of academic paper writing — from initial reading and style analysis, through drafting and revision, to pre-submission review simulation.

Each skill encodes deep domain expertise: modeling scientific reasoning patterns, reverse-engineering argumentation strategies, amplifying novelty, and simulating rigorous peer review. These skills operate at the level of **scientific thinking**, not surface-level language polishing.

### The Five Skills

| # | Skill | Chapters | Core Role | When to Use |
|---|-------|----------|-----------|-------------|
| 1 | **Academic Paper Writing** | 8 | Foundational standards + De-AI-ification + Citation transparency + Polishing | Entire workflow |

| 4 | **Reviewer Simulation & Rejection Prediction Engine** | 6 | Simulate peer review & predict rejection risks | Before submission |
| 5 | **Top-Journal Writing Engine** | 7 | Maximize publishability + Multi-domain journal profiles | Writing / Revision |

### Key Capabilities

**De-AI-ification System** (§5 of Academic Paper Writing) — The sole owner of AI-pattern detection and removal:
- Em dash prohibition (zero tolerance)
- Bullet point & list prohibition in body text
- Mechanical connector suppression (Firstly/Secondly/Finally)
- High-frequency AI vocabulary replacement (Tier 1–3, 70+ words)
- Structural pattern detection (Not only...but also, empty intensifiers, hedge cascades)
- Language-specific patterns (English + Chinese)
- 8-point self-check protocol

**Citation Transparency** (§4 of Academic Paper Writing) — Full citation change tracking:
- Change tracking log (additions / deletions / preservations)
- Mandatory justification for every citation change
- References synchronization check
- Anti-fabrication rules
- Citation preservation principle (default: keep)

**Multi-Domain Journal Profiles** (§5 of Top-Journal Writing) — 7 domains, 24+ venues:
- Multidisciplinary: Nature, Science, PNAS, Nature Communications, Science Advances
- Biology & Ecology: Current Biology, eLife, Ecology Letters, Biological Conservation, Methods in Ecology and Evolution, Behavioral Ecology, Journal of Animal Ecology, Conservation Biology
- Bioinformatics: Bioinformatics, PLOS Computational Biology, Briefings in Bioinformatics
- Computer Science & AI: IEEE TPAMI, CVPR/ICCV/ECCV, NeurIPS/ICML/ICLR
- Engineering & Signal Processing: IEEE TSP, JASA, IEEE/ACM TASLP
- Medical Imaging: Medical Image Analysis, IEEE TMI
- Geospatial & Remote Sensing: ISPRS Journal, Remote Sensing of Environment

Each venue profile includes reviewer focus areas and preferred keywords.

### Core Philosophy

- **Logic over language** — Scientific writing is fundamentally logical argumentation, not prose decoration
- **Evidence-driven** — Every claim must be supported by data, literature, or rigorous reasoning
- **Process over imitation** — Model the *thinking* behind great papers, not their word choices
- **Publishability as the north star** — Every writing decision serves the goal of acceptance at top venues

### 核心能力

**去 AI 味系统**（Academic Paper Writing §5，唯一负责）：
- 破折号禁令（零容忍）
- 项目符号与列表禁止（正文中）
- 机械化连接词抑制（Firstly/Secondly/Finally 等）
- 高频 AI 词汇替换表（Tier 1–3，70+ 词汇）
- 句式结构模式检测（Not only...but also 陷阱、空泛强调词、Hedge 堆叠等）
- 中英文分语言专项
- 8 条自查协议

**引用透明原则**（Academic Paper Writing §4，唯一负责）：
- 引用变更追踪日志（新增/删减/保留）
- 每条增删必须有明确学术理由
- References 同步检查
- 反虚构规则
- 引用保留原则（默认保留已有引用）

**多领域期刊画像**（Top-Journal Writing §5，唯一负责）—— 7 大领域，24+ 种期刊：
- 综合科学：Nature, Science, PNAS, Nature Communications, Science Advances
- 生物与生态：Current Biology, eLife, Ecology Letters, Biological Conservation, Methods in Ecology and Evolution, Behavioral Ecology, Journal of Animal Ecology, Conservation Biology
- 生物信息学：Bioinformatics, PLOS Computational Biology, Briefings in Bioinformatics
- 计算机科学：IEEE TPAMI, CVPR/ICCV/ECCV, NeurIPS/ICML/ICLR
- 工程与信号处理：IEEE TSP, JASA, IEEE/ACM TASLP
- 医学影像：Medical Image Analysis, IEEE TMI
- 遥感与地理空间：ISPRS Journal, Remote Sensing of Environment

每种期刊含审稿人关注点和推荐关键词。

### 核心理念

- **逻辑优先，语言其次** — 学术写作本质上是逻辑论证过程，而非文采修饰
- **证据驱动** — 每个重要陈述必须由数据、文献或严密推理支撑
- **过程复现而非措辞模仿** — 建模的是优秀论文背后的**科学思维**，而非其用词选择
- **以可发表性为北极星** — 每个写作决策都服务于在顶级期刊上获得接收的目标
