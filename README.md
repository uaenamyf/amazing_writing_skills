## English

**Amazing Writing Skills** is a comprehensive collection of 5 specialized AI-agent skills designed to assist researchers through every stage of academic paper writing — from initial reading and style analysis, through drafting and revision, to pre-submission review simulation.

Rather than generic writing assistance, each skill encodes deep domain expertise: modeling scientific reasoning patterns, reverse-engineering argumentation strategies, amplifying novelty, and simulating rigorous peer review. The skills operate at the level of **scientific thinking**, not surface-level language polishing.

### The Five Skills

| # | Skill | Role | When to Use |
|---|-------|------|-------------|
| 1 | **Academic Paper Writing** | Foundational writing standards | Entire workflow |
| 2 | **Author Style Fingerprint Engine** | Analyze & replicate authorial reasoning patterns | Before writing |
| 3 | **Paper Reverse Engineering** | Extract underlying writing strategies from papers | Reading / Analysis |
| 4 | **Reviewer Simulation & Rejection Prediction Engine** | Simulate peer review & predict rejection risks | Before submission |
| 5 | **Top-Journal Writing Engine** | Maximize publishability for elite venues | Writing / Revision |

### Core Philosophy

- **Logic over language** — Scientific writing is fundamentally logical argumentation, not prose decoration
- **Evidence-driven** — Every claim must be supported by data, literature, or rigorous reasoning
- **Process over imitation** — Model the *thinking* behind great papers, not their word choices
- **Publishability as the north star** — Every writing decision serves the goal of acceptance at top venues

## 中文

**Amazing Writing Skills（学术写作智能体技能集）** 是一套面向学术论文写作全流程的专业化 AI 智能体技能集合，共包含 5 个技能模块，覆盖从前期阅读与风格分析、到撰写与修改、再到投稿前审稿模拟的完整链路。

与通用写作辅助不同，每个技能模块都编码了深层的领域专长：建模科学推理模式、逆向工程论证策略、放大学术新颖性、模拟严格同行评审。这些技能工作在**科学思维层面**，而非表层的语言润色。

### 五大技能概览

| 序号 | 技能名称 | 核心定位 | 适用阶段 |
|------|----------|----------|----------|
| 1 | **Academic Paper Writing**（学术论文写作基础规范） | 全流程学术写作基础规范，遵循逻辑优先、证据驱动、客观科学语调三大核心原则 | 全流程 |
| 2 | **Author Style Fingerprint Engine**（作者风格指纹引擎） | 学习并复现特定作者/研究组的科学思维过程与论证策略 | 写作前 |
| 3 | **Paper Reverse Engineering**（论文逆向工程） | 对目标论文进行底层写作策略逆向工程，提取推理模式与修辞结构 | 阅读/分析 |
| 4 | **Reviewer Simulation & Rejection Prediction Engine**（审稿人模拟与拒稿预测引擎） | 模拟顶级期刊审稿委员会，从新颖性、方法论、实验、影响力四维度预判拒稿风险 | 投稿前 |
| 5 | **Top-Journal Writing Engine**（顶级期刊写作引擎） | 以最大化可发表性为目标，构建完整科学叙事链 | 写作/修改 |

### 核心理念

- **逻辑优先，语言其次** — 学术写作本质上是逻辑论证过程，而非文采修饰
- **证据驱动** — 每个重要陈述必须由数据、文献或严密推理支撑
- **过程复现而非措辞模仿** — 建模的是优秀论文背后的**科学思维**，而非其用词选择
- **以可发表性为北极星** — 每个写作决策都服务于在顶级期刊/会议上获得接收的目标
